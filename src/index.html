The Aboitiz Scholars Central app is a scholarship dashboard that aims to be the central hub for everything related to scholarships. Its dashboard will provide information on the total number of supported scholars, their scholarship status, the number of graduates, their employment status, grades, and demographics like age, gender, and location.
